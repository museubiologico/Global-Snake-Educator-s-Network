ENGLISH
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Global Snake Educators Network repository aims to share resources that 
strengthen education, support conservation, and raise global awareness about 
snakebites to improve snake understanding and reduce human-wildlife conflict.

This is a joint initiative of the Butantan Biological Museum and SAVE THE SNAKES.
For more information or to collaborate, visit: 
https://sites.google.com/savethesnakes.org/global-snake-educators-network

HOW TO NAVIGATE AND ACCESS THIS REPOSITORY:
1. Click on "Branches" and choose one of the following options:
   - Education Materials: School presentations, lesson plans, educational 
     activities, ebooks, and didactic materials.
   - Outreach Tools: Posters, infographics, community awareness materials, 
     and informative folders.
   - Snakebite Prevention: First aid awareness and community safety guides.
   - Snake Identification: Regional species guides and illustrated materials.
2. After accessing the branch, download the document by clicking the download 
   icon (arrow pointing down) in the right corner.

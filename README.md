# Global-Snake-Educator-s-Network
The Global Snake Educators Network repository aims to share resources that strengthens education, supports conservation, and raises global awareness about snakebites to improve snake understanding and reduce human-wildlife conflict. 

This is a joint initiative of Butantan Biological Museum and Save the Snakes.

If you want to collaborate with our network or access more information, please connect to the project website:
https://sites.google.com/savethesnakes.org/global-snake-educators-network?usp=sharing

How to navigate and acess this repository:

Click in "Branches" and choose one of the following options. After acess the branch, you can download the document of interest by clicking in the right corner (arrow pointing down).

Education Materials
- school presentations
- lesson plans
- educational activities
- ebooks or didatic materials for students and professors
  
Outreach Tools
- posters and infographics
- community awareness materials
- informative folders
  
Snakebite Prevention
- first aid awareness materials
- community safety guides

Snake Identification
- regional species guides
- educational materials with photos and illustrations
